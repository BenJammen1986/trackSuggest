At Epicodus, we believe that every person:


  • Has the ability to learn programming, and
  • Should be given the opportunity to learn programming if they so desire-
    regardless of their background or financial situation.

That is why all our materials are available online for free!

With that being said:


It's important for YOU to make sure that you fully understand:

  1. The commitment that you are making (time, money, energy, etc...)
  2. The type of mindset it takes to become a successful developer.

If you are just looking at Indeed or Monster or Careerbuilder and see all the opportunities/income potential within programming - and there ARE many opportunities available - you may need to take a step back and re-evaluate.

We hope you understand: We aren't trying to discourage you!

On the contrary, we simply believe that it's important for you to have a BIG WHY: to know exactly why you've chosen to make this change in your life, and your purpose/goals that you want to accomplish once you finish. For most people, their driving motivation for completing a challenging, monumental task has to be a little deeper than simply "I can make XYZ dollars doing it"! That's probably not going to get you through the intensity of this program.

If you choose to come to Epicodus, we want you to be completely committed and focused, so that you can finish well and be not only successful in graduating, but be thoroughly satisfied with the career you've chosen!

So: your first step in your journey is to go through the questionnaire below and answer the questions as honestly as you can.
